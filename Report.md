## Формулы для рассчетов:
$$
\omega_0 = \frac{1}{\sqrt{LC}}
$$
$$
T=2\pi\sqrt{LC}
$$
$$
\nu_T = \frac{1}{T}
$$
$$
Q_T = \frac{1}{R}\sqrt{\frac{L}{C}}
$$
$$
\delta = \frac{R}{2L}
$$
